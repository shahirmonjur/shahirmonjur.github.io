Thesis Summary

Task Offloading Optimization in Vehicular Edge Computing Network Using ML

B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering | Green University of Bangladesh | 2025

Proposed a robust strategy for task offloading in vehicular edge networks to maximize utilization of network edge resources in busy urban areas. The model introduces UAVs and parked user vehicles into the offloading scenario as task processing nodes to leverage distributed compute near the edge.

The approach aims to minimize workload on edge servers while optimizing decision making for minimal delay and energy consumption under budget constraints, improving resilience and performance of urban edge systems.
